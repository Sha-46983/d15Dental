D15 Dental Care New furbished Modern dental clinic

Located in the Tyrellstown Plaza, the D15 Dental Care Clinic has a highly modern infrastructure and top quality equipment, its own sterilization room and follows all the hygienic procedures and protocols for the safety of the patients. We have our own state of the art CBCT machine, 3D printer for oral appliances and Intra oral scanner for greater comfort of patient, making our clinic very unique in the area. we also provide services based on PRSI entitlements and welcome patients with Vhi & Decare Insurance companies.
